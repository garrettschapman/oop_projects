roman_to_arabic
Garrett Chapman

roman_to_arabic converts Roman numerals to Arabic numerals and vice versa.

Entering an Arabic numeral between 1 and 3999 in the box labeled "Arabic Numeral" will cause its Roman numeral counterpart to appear in the box below it.
Entering a Roman numeral between I and MMMCMXCIX in the box labeled "Roman Numeral" will cause its Arabic numeral counterpart to appear in the box above it.

The program does not allow inputs outside of these ranges, and will delete them automatically.
There are no buttons that need to be clicked for the conversion.
